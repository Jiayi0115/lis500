Lisa Su: Leading AMD into the Future

Lisa Su is the CEO and President of AMD (Advanced Micro Devices), a global leader in the semiconductor industry. Under her leadership, AMD has become a major force in the development of high-performance computing, graphics, and visualization technologies.

Lisa Su has transformed AMD’s business strategy, leading to record revenue growth and innovation in chip design, including the development of the AMD Ryzen processors and EPYC server chips. These advancements have cemented AMD as a leader in the semiconductor market.

"Success is not about where you start but where you reach," says Lisa Su. Her vision and leadership have inspired many, particularly women and minorities in the technology industry.

Career Timeline of Lisa Su

1994

Graduated with a Ph.D. from MIT in Electrical Engineering.

2007

Joined Freescale Semiconductor as Chief Technology Officer.

2012

Joined AMD as Senior Vice President and General Manager of Global Business Units.

2014

Became the President and CEO of AMD, leading the company's strategic turnaround.

2021

Under Lisa's leadership, AMD achieved record financial results, challenging major competitors.

About AMD

Founded in 1969, AMD is known for developing innovative computing and graphics technologies. Under Lisa Su’s leadership, AMD has focused on high-performance products and has seen major advancements in its processor lineup, challenging its competitors and pushing the industry forward.

Lisa Su’s contributions to the technology world have earned her numerous accolades, including being named one of the most powerful women in business by Forbes.
