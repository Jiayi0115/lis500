Yun Yao: Innovating Predictive Maintenance

Yun Yao is the co-founder and CEO of Soralink, a company revolutionizing predictive maintenance through advanced AI sensor technology. Soralink's technology predicts when a machine or piece of equipment will need maintenance before a breakdown occurs, significantly reducing costs, waste, and enhancing worker safety.

Yun Yao’s inspiration to start Soralink came from her experience leading innovations in haptic and wireless systems. She believes that the integration of AI, IoT, and wireless technologies represents a powerful transformative force. The goal of Soralink is to harness this force to enhance the manufacturing sector through predictive maintenance.

"Predictive maintenance on critical machines does more than just cut costs and reduce waste—it also substantially improves worker safety," says Yun Yao.
